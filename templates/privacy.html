Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Who We Are

World Cup 2026 Prediction Game is a friendly prediction competition available on the web and as a mobile app. For any privacy questions, contact us at jalankmd@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

When you create an account we collect your first and last name, a username, your email address, and a password (stored only as a secure hash). While you play, we store the predictions you make (match scores, qualifiers, and podium picks), your points, and the groups you belong to.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or any data from your device beyond what is listed above. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to run the prediction game: signing you in, recording your predictions, calculating scores, and showing leaderboards and predictions to other members of the groups you join. Your name and username are visible to other players in your groups; your email address and password are never shown to anyone.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely on our hosting provider (Railway) and transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the mobile app under More → Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing jalankmd@gmail.com. Deletion removes your account, predictions, and scores immediately and cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and revise the date above.
