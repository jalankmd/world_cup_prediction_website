Prediction deadlines

  • Match predictions — lock at kick-off. Once a match starts you cannot change your score.
  • Group qualifier picks — lock when the first match in that group kicks off.
  • Podium picks — lock when the very first match of the tournament kicks off.

Competition 2: Odds Predictor

How to earn points

Pick the result of each match: Home win, Draw, or Away win.

OutcomePoints
Correct pickThe displayed odds for that result (e.g. 2.50 pts)
Wrong pick0 pts

Points are decimal — the riskier the pick, the higher the reward.

Prediction deadlines

  • Match predictions — lock at kick-off. Odds are fixed at the time you save your pick.

Example

If Morocco is listed at 3.40 odds to win and they do win, you earn 3.40 pts for that match.

General Rules

  • You must have a valid group code to register.
  • Each player competes independently — no team play.
  • The admin enters all official results. Points are updated shortly after each match ends.
  • Locked predictions cannot be changed, even if there is a technical issue.
  • The player with the most points at the end of the tournament wins each competition.
